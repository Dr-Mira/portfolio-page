Robotic Liquid Handler

From a refurbished 3D printer. The racks, base, pipette holder, calibration tabs, are custom designed and 3D printed. The pipette itself is a commercial analytical 1 mL pipette. Machine kinetics system has sub 0.1 mm accuracy, and thanks to the pipette, the liquid accuracy and precision are both about 1%, which is on par with state-of-the-art machines.

Supports up to 3×96 well plates, Eppis, 15/50 mL Falcon tubes, and 4 mL vials. Includes liquid-air eject (for remaining liquid), reliable tip pick/eject, aspirate–dispense mixing, and volatile-friendly transport offsets for solvents such as ACN and MeOH.

Motion and liquid routines are driven by a Python control script with a custom user interface that sends G-code over serial. It also manages calibration/config values from config json, giving a practical balance of precision control and easy tuning.
