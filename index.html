About Me

I'm a Ph.D. organic chemist with a passion for bridging traditional wet-lab chemistry with modern automation and instrumentation. Currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Colorado Boulder in the Marvin Caruthers Lab, I specialize in oligonucleotide synthesis and lab automation.

My research spans solid-phase synthesis, medicinal chemistry, and advanced analytics. I've co-led initiatives that scaled oligonucleotide production 5×, and I've designed and built multiple custom research instruments from scratch.

I'm relocating to Czechia in early 2027 and seeking opportunities where synthetic chemistry, automation, and instrument prototyping intersect.
